    This calculator has two interfaces, one is basic and the other is advance. In basic mode you are only limited to addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.
    In advance mode you have more options. You would notice that in basic mode, the calculator works as a simple porpo calculator and the every anser is truncated. Also the '.' button does not work in basic mode. Basic mode is the requirements from the course instructor. Advance is just an extra feature.
